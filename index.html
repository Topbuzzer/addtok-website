What is Addtok?
Ads you actually want to see

Addtok is a short-form feed built entirely for ads and product promotions. Scroll through the best ads from brands, influencers and creators — and tap directly through to buy.

▶
Short-form ad feed
Scroll through video ads just like TikTok — but every single one is a product you can buy. Swipe, discover, shop.
🔗
Direct product links
Every ad has a direct link to the product website. One tap takes you straight to the product page — no searching needed.
👍
Community voting
Vote on the best ads. The community decides what rises to the top — only the most genuine, creative ads get seen most.
🏆
Weekly leaderboards
The most voted ads of the week win the top spots. Brands compete to make the best ads — you benefit from better content.
🎯
Browse by category
Filter by fashion, tech, beauty, fitness, food, gaming and more. Only see ads for things you actually care about.
🛍️
Merch drops & exclusives
Limited edition drops, early access offers, and exclusive deals — all in one place, before they sell out.
⚔️
Ad Battles
Two ads go head to head — the community votes for the winner. The winning ad earns featured placement for 24 hours. Only on Addtok.
⭐
Ad of the Day
Every 24 hours the community crowns one ad as the best of the day. Brands compete hard for the crown — you get the best content.
⭐
Points & Streaks
Earn Addtok points every time you vote, react, save or visit a product. Build daily streaks, climb the leaderboard and win prizes.
▶
Discovery Mode
Full-screen swipe mode — flick through ads one by one, vote, save or visit with a single tap. The most immersive way to shop.
How It Works
Simple as scroll, tap, buy

Addtok connects consumers with the products they love in three simple steps.

1
Browse the ad feed
Scroll through short video ads from top brands, influencers and creators. Filter by category to find what you love.
2
Vote on your favourites
Upvote ads you love, downvote ones that miss the mark. Your votes shape the leaderboard and help the best ads rise to the top.
3
Tap the link and buy
Every ad has a direct link to the product. One tap takes you straight to the brand's website to buy — no extra searching.
4
Earn points and rewards
Vote, react, and discover products to earn Addtok points. Climb the leaderboard and unlock rewards every week.
