What is Addtok?
Ads you actually want to see

Addtok is a TikTok-style feed built entirely for advertisements and product promotions. Scroll through the best ads from your favourite brands, influencers, and creators — and tap directly through to buy.

▶
Short-form ad feed
Scroll through video ads just like TikTok — but every single one is a product you can buy. Swipe, discover, shop.
🔗
Direct product links
Every ad has a direct link to the product website. One tap takes you straight to the product page, no searching needed.
👍
Community voting
Vote on the best ads. The community decides what rises to the top — only the most genuine, creative ads get seen most.
🏆
Weekly leaderboards
The most voted ads of the week win the top spots. Brands compete to make the best ads — you benefit from better content.
🎯
Browse by category
Filter by fashion, tech, beauty, fitness, food, gaming and more. Only see ads for things you actually care about.
🛍️
Merch drops & exclusives
Limited edition drops, exclusive discount codes, and early access offers — all in one place, before they sell out.
How It Works
Simple as scroll, tap, buy

Addtok connects consumers with the products they love in three simple steps.

1
Browse the ad feed
Scroll through short video and image ads from top brands, influencers, and creators. Filter by category to find what you love.
2
Vote on your favourites
Upvote ads you love, downvote ones that miss the mark. Your votes shape the leaderboard and help the best ads rise to the top.
3
Tap the link and buy
Every ad has a direct link to the product. One tap takes you straight to the brand's website to buy — no extra searching.
4
Grab exclusive deals
Many ads include exclusive discount codes, early access links, and limited edition drops available only through Addtok.

Payments
How payments work

Simple and transparent — whether you're a shopper or an advertiser.

🛍️
For Shoppers
Browsing and buying on Addtok
FREE
✓ Completely free to browse, vote, and discover ads on Addtok — no account needed.
✓ Tap any ad to go directly to the brand's own website or product page.
✓ Pay the brand directly on their website — Addtok never touches your payment or card details.
📢
For Advertisers
Running ads on Addtok
PAY AS YOU GO
✓ You only pay per click — every time a user taps your product link and lands on your website, you pay Addtok a small fee. No click, no charge.
✓ No monthly subscription — there are no fixed costs. Your budget is entirely controlled by how many clicks your ad receives.
✓ Set a daily cap — choose how much you want to spend per day. When your cap is reached, your ad pauses automatically.
✓ Cost per click est. £0.30–£1.20 — competitive UK market rate. Final pricing confirmed at launch.
✓ Powered by Stripe — secure card payments, instant billing, full transaction history in your dashboard.
